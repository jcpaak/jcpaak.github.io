Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Rice
  • Spam
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Seaweed
  • Sesame Seeds

Directions

  1. Heat up the pan
  2. Cook the spam til its got golden brown edges
  3. Toss in the kimchi and add in some kimchi juice
  4. Put in the rice and mix it all together
  5. While mixing, add in soy sauce and sesame oil
  6. Shred up some seaweed sprinkle it on top with sesame seeds
  7. Finally, fry an egg and put it on top of your fried rice!
